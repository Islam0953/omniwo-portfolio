## SLIDE 2: The Problem
- **Blood tests are confusing** — you get numbers but no context, no action plan
- **Health data is siloed** — blood results in one app, wearable data in another, GP notes elsewhere
- **Preventative health is reactive** — people only act when something goes wrong
- **Existing platforms (Thriva, Medichecks)** show results but don't tell you what they mean or what to do
- 60% of chronic diseases are preventable through lifestyle changes, yet people lack actionable guidance

---

## SLIDE 3: The Solution
**Omniwo** is a Longevity Intelligence Platform that turns blood test data + wearable data into a clear, actionable health roadmap.

Users order blood tests (home kit or nurse visit) → results appear in a personal dashboard with plain-language explanations → the platform correlates blood biomarkers with wearable data (WHOOP, Fitbit, Google Fit) → delivers personalised protocols to optimise healthspan.

**Not diagnosis. Not fear. Motivation and clarity.**

---

## SLIDE 4: Product — Live MVP
**Blood Testing**
- 73 test panels available (£89–£299)
- Home collection kits or nurse-to-door service
- Lab partner: Inuvi (UK-accredited)

**Intelligent Dashboard**
- Plain language: "Your stress hormone (Cortisol) is elevated" — not "Cortisol 580 nmol/L"
- Bio Age — biological age calculated from biomarkers
- Health Score — unified score combining blood + wearable + lifestyle data
- Correlations — "Your HRV correlates with your iron levels"
- Predictions — "If you continue this trend, in 12 months your cholesterol will be X"

**Personalised Protocols**
- Step-by-step: Food → Lifestyle → Supplements → Doctor (only when needed)
- Natural approaches first — no pharma recommendations
- Wellness service, not medical diagnosis

**Wearable Integration**
- WHOOP, Fitbit, Google Fit connected
- Continuous data layered onto blood biomarker trends
